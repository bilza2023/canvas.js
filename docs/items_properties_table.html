Canvas Items Properties

This page lists all item types available in TaleemCanvas and their customizable properties.

1. Circle

#PropertyDescription
1colorFill color.
2filledFilled or outlined.
3lineWidthStroke thickness.
4dashDash pattern.
5gapDash gap.
6globalAlphaOpacity.

2. Ellipse

#PropertyDescription
1colorFill or stroke color.
2filledDetermines if the ellipse is filled.
3lineWidthThickness of the stroke.
4radiusXHorizontal radius.
5radiusYVertical radius.
6globalAlphaOpacity (0 to 1).

3. ImageItem

#PropertyDescription
1srcImage file name.
2globalAlphaOpacity (0 to 1).

4. Line

#PropertyDescription
1colorLine color.
2lineWidthThickness of the line.
3dashDash pattern length.
4gapGap between dashes.
5globalAlphaOpacity (0 to 1).

5. List

#PropertyDescription
1listArrayArray of text items.
2fontSizeText size.
3fontFamilyFont style.
4colorText color.
5lineGapSpacing between list items.
6indentationIndentation for nested items.
7globalAlphaOpacity (0 to 1).

6. Pie Chart

#PropertyDescription
1radiusPie chart size.
2dataArray of slices.
3showLabelsShow labels.
4labelFontSizeFont size of labels.
5labelColorLabel color.
6globalAlphaOpacity (0 to 1).

7. Ray

#PropertyDescription
1colorRay line color.
2lineWidthThickness of the ray line.
3startArrowShow/hide start arrow.
4endArrowShow/hide end arrow.
5arrowWidthWidth of the arrowhead.
6arrowHeightHeight of the arrowhead.
7globalAlphaOpacity (0 to 1).

8. Rectangle

#PropertyDescription
1colorFill color.
2filledFilled or outlined.
3lineWidthStroke thickness.
4dashDash pattern.
5gapDash gap.
6globalAlphaOpacity.

9. Sprite

#PropertyDescription
1srcSprite sheet source.
2selectedItemDisplayed sprite.
3globalAlphaOpacity.

10. Triangle

#PropertyDescription
1colorFill or outline color.
2filledFilled or outlined.
3lineWidthStroke thickness.
4globalAlphaOpacity (0 to 1).
